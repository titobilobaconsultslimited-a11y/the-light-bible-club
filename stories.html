Bible Stories 📖

Amazing, true stories of faith, miracles, and God's incredible love for His people — told just for kids!

🌈
Genesis 6–9 · Old Testament

Noah and the Ark 🚢

God saw that the world was full of wickedness, but Noah was a righteous man who walked faithfully with God. God told Noah to build a huge ark and fill it with animals — two of every kind! It rained for 40 days and 40 nights. When the waters dried up, God placed a beautiful rainbow in the sky as His promise never to flood the whole earth again.

"I have set my rainbow in the clouds, and it will be the sign of the covenant between me and the earth." — Genesis 9:13
Lesson: Obey God even when it seems strange to others. God rewards faithfulness!
🗿
1 Samuel 17 · Old Testament

David and Goliath ⚔️

A giant named Goliath terrified the entire Israelite army. He was over 9 feet tall! But young David, a shepherd boy, was not afraid. He trusted in God and ran toward Goliath with a sling and five smooth stones. With one stone, the giant fell. David's courage came not from his own strength but from his deep faith in God Almighty.

"The Lord who rescued me from the paw of the lion and the paw of the bear will rescue me from the hand of this Philistine." — 1 Samuel 17:37
Lesson: No challenge is too big when God is on your side. Be brave and trust Him!
🦁
Daniel 6 · Old Testament

Daniel in the Lion's Den 🙏

Daniel was a faithful man who prayed to God three times a day. When jealous officials convinced the king to make praying to anyone but the king illegal, Daniel still prayed. He was thrown into a den of hungry lions! But God sent an angel to shut the lions' mouths. Daniel came out without a scratch, and the king praised God because of his miracle.

"My God sent his angel, and he shut the mouths of the lions. They have not hurt me, because I was found innocent in his sight." — Daniel 6:22
Lesson: Never stop praying, no matter what. God protects those who trust in Him.
🐟
Jonah 1–4 · Old Testament

Jonah and the Big Fish 🌊

God told Jonah to go to the city of Nineveh and warn the people about their sin. But Jonah ran away on a ship instead! A massive storm came, and Jonah was thrown overboard. A giant fish swallowed him! Inside the fish for three days, Jonah prayed and repented. The fish spat him out, and Jonah finally went to Nineveh — and the whole city turned to God!

"When my life was ebbing away, I remembered you, Lord, and my prayer rose to you." — Jonah 2:7
Lesson: You can't hide from God! When we repent and obey, He gives us a second chance.
🍞
John 6:1–14 · New Testament

Jesus Feeds 5,000 People 🐟

A huge crowd of over 5,000 people followed Jesus to hear Him teach. They were very hungry but had no food. A young boy offered his small lunch — five loaves of bread and two fish. Jesus took the food, gave thanks to God, and began breaking it. Amazingly, it never ran out! Everyone ate until they were full, and there were 12 baskets of leftovers!

"Here is a boy with five small barley loaves and two small fish, but how far will they go among so many?" — John 6:9
Lesson: When we give what little we have to Jesus, He can do the impossible with it!
💛
Luke 15:11–32 · New Testament

The Prodigal Son 🏠

A young son asked his father for his inheritance early and wasted it all on wild living. He ended up feeding pigs and eating their food! He came to his senses and returned home, ready to be a servant. But his father saw him from afar, ran to him, and threw a huge party to celebrate his return. This is a picture of how God welcomes us back when we turn to Him.

"For this son of mine was dead and is alive again; he was lost and is found." — Luke 15:24
Lesson: God's love never gives up on us. No matter what, we can always return to Him.
🌊
Exodus 14 · Old Testament

Moses and the Red Sea 🪄

God's people, the Israelites, were trapped between Pharaoh's army and the Red Sea. There was nowhere to run! Moses stretched out his hand, and God parted the sea — creating a dry path right through the middle! All the people walked across safely. When Pharaoh's army followed, the water crashed back and they were defeated. God had rescued His people again!

"The Lord will fight for you; you need only to be still." — Exodus 14:14
Lesson: When we face impossible situations, God makes a way where there seems to be no way!
⭐
Luke 2:1–20 · New Testament

The Birth of Jesus 🎄

God's greatest gift to the world — Jesus! Mary and Joseph travelled to Bethlehem, and because there was no room at the inn, Jesus was born in a humble stable. Angels appeared to shepherds in the fields, saying, "Do not be afraid — the Saviour has been born!" The shepherds ran to find Jesus lying in a manger. Wise men followed a star from the East to worship Him too.

"Today in the town of David a Savior has been born to you; he is the Messiah, the Lord." — Luke 2:11
Lesson: God loves us so much He sent His Son Jesus to save the world!
🙌
Acts 16:16–34 · New Testament

Paul and Silas in Prison 🎵

Paul and Silas were thrown into prison for preaching the gospel. Their feet were locked in stocks. But instead of crying or giving up, they sang praises to God at midnight! Suddenly a great earthquake shook the prison, all the doors flew open, and everyone's chains fell off! The amazed jailer and his whole family became believers that very night.

"About midnight Paul and Silas were praying and singing hymns to God, and the other prisoners were listening to them." — Acts 16:25
Lesson: Praise God even in hard times — your worship can break chains and change everything!
