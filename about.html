Discover our story, our mission, and the wonderful people who make this club special.

Who We Are

The Light Bible Club is a children's ministry dedicated to teaching God's Word in a fun, safe, and age-appropriate environment. We believe every child deserves to know how much God loves them.

We gather every Saturday to explore Bible stories, worship together, and build lifelong friendships rooted in faith. Our teachers are trained, passionate, and deeply care about each child's spiritual growth.

Whether your child is hearing about Jesus for the first time or has grown up in church, there is a place for them here at The Light Bible Club!
